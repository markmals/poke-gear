Like all Pokémon games, there's a myriad of Legendary Pokémon to collect. Some are available throughout Sinnoh while others require you to go through Ramanas Park. Like prior Pokémon games, they will return if you defeat them without capture and then beat the Elite Four

## Palkia

![](https://static1.thegamerimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/pokemon-brilliant-diamond-shining-pearl-spear-pillar-dialga-palkia.jpg)

**Location**: Spear Pillar

You will encounter Palkia as you progress through the story. As Team Galactic's plan unfolds through the Sinnoh region, you will make chase to Spear Pillar where Cyrus will unleash his plans to create a new world. However, when you defeat him, Uxie, Mesprit and Azelf will appear and free Palkia, and it will then want to battle you.

## Giratina

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/giratina-ow.jpg)

**Location**: Turnback Cave

After you get the National Dex, you'll be given access to the Sendoff Spring, a mysterious fourth lake to the east of Route 214. In this lake you will fin Turnback Cave. This cave is a winding maze that gives you instructions on the pillar. Your task is to get to the end in 30 rooms or less. The pillar gives you a specific number and that number equates to the clockwise exit you need to take on entering each room. If you follow the instructions, you will be find Giratina in its Altered Forme.

## Ramanas Park

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/ramanaspark.jpg)

Ramanas Park is a new area in Pokémon Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl. This area replaces the Pal Park and can be found on Route 221 after you have completed the Sinnoh Pokédex and is different in that there are various caves dotted around the park. These caves allow for you to get Legendary Pokémon. To do that, you need to get special slates to then put into the pedestal to summon the Pokémon.

When you put the slates in the pedestal, you get transported to the Pure Space for Sub-Legendary Pokémon and the Strange Space for Legendary Pokémon. The Legendary Pokémon will have Hidden Abilities if they have one

When you have caught the Pokémon, you will also then get given a statue of them for your Secret Base in the Grand Underground.
