Like all Pokémon games, there's a myriad of Pokémon you'll find in the overworld. These Pokémon are exclusive to these methods so can only be found here, some on specific days or times.

## Turtwig, Chimchar, Piplup

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/starters-ow.jpg)

**Location**: Lake Verity

The first Pokémon you get is a choice between the Starter Pokémon. When you enter Lake Verity with Barry, you will see Professor Rowan who will quickly leave, but leaving his briefcase. When you spot the briefcase, some Starly appear and attack causing you to have to pick the Pokémon you desire.

## Happiny

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/happiny-ow.jpg)

**Location**: Hearthome City

When you go to leave Hearthome City in the bottom right, the man by the gate will ask if you'd like to take a Pokémon egg. If you say yes, you'll get the egg which hatches into a Happiny.

## Riolu

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/riolu-ow.jpg)

**Location**: Iron Island

After you have helped Riley through Iron Island to investigate what is causing the Pokémon to act violently in the area, he will ask if you'd like to take a Pokémon egg. If you say yes, you'll get the egg which hatches into a Riolu.

## Eevee

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/eevee-ow.jpg)

**Location**: Hearthome City

After you have obtained the National Pokédex, speak to Bebe in her house in Hearthome City and she will give you an Eevee that she got from a friend who lives in Johto.

## Fossil Pokémon

![](https://www.serebii.net/brilliantdiamondshiningpearl/fossils-ow.jpg)

**Location**: Oreburgh City

In the Grand Underground, you have the ability to dig up a variety of fossils, if you take these fossils to the Oreburgh City museum, a man will restore them into a Pokémon for you. This is done one at a time and you need to exit the building and re-enter in order to get it.
